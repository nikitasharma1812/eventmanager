Tent rentals

We have a large variety of tent types at Aztec Events & Tents. Our tents can be staked, anchored in concrete or anchored with water barrels or concrete blocks. These tent types can have additional rentals inside of them, such as lighting, flooring, décor, draping and much more to upgrade your event.

Frame Tent

Frame tents are preferred due to their free-standing design as the legs are solely around the perimeter. Frame tents may be used with or without sidewalls and can be built in 10’ increments. Frame tents are a more affordable option and ideal for backyard parties, social gatherings, cook-offs, company parties or sporting events.

Structure Tent

Structure tents are preferred due to their free-standing design as the legs are solely around the perimeter. Structure tents may be used with or without sidewalls and can be built in 10’ increments. Structure tents resemble a more permanent structure as the fabric of the tent is supported by rigid frames instead of poles. Structure tents are ideal for weddings, disaster relief, long-term installations or climate-controlled events.

Marquee Tent

Marquee tents are preferred due to their high peak and free-standing design as the legs are solely around the perimeter. Marquee tents may be used with or without sidewalls and are available in 10’x10’, 10’x20’ and 20’x20’. The marquee tent tops are available in solid white or red/white stripe, making them easily visible amongst other tents. Marquee tents are ideal for festivals or small backyard events.

Pole Tent

Pole tents are preferred due to the high peaks as the legs are down the center and around the perimeter. Pole tents must be staked into the grass or drilled into concrete. The Tidewater Sailcloth top is available in select sizes. Pole tents are ideal for weddings, festivals or ranch events.

Double-Decker Tent

Double-Decker tents are preferred due to their two-tiered structure that allows you to double your available square footage. Double-Decker tents are built with the same modular components as our structure tents and available in various widths, heights, and lengths. Double-Decker tents may be transformed to include internal and external balconies and stairways. Double-Decker tents are ideal for corporate gatherings, festivals or sporting events.p>

Triple-Decker Tent

Triple-Decker tents are preferred due to their three-tiered structure that allows you to triple your available square footage. Triple-Decker tents are built with the same modular components as our Double-Decker tent. Aztec Events & Tents is the first company in North America to debut the Triple-Decker tent, which was showcased at the 2015 Breeder’s Cup. Triple-Decker tents are ideal for corporate gatherings, festivals or sporting events.
